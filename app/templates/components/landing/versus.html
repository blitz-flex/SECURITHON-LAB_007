Why Securithon is Different

Compare our architectural approach against legacy paradigms.

Traditional CTFs

  • Focuses only on breaking things.
  • Unrealistic "flag hiding" scenarios.
  • No infrastructure context.
  • Does not teach how to patch or write secure code.

Securithon Lab

  • Simulates full production architectures.
  • Requires you to write actual code to fix vulnerabilities.
  • Automated CI/CD regression testing on your patches.
  • Trains you for both Red Team and Blue Team operations.

Corporate Training

  • Boring multiple-choice video courses.
  • No hands-on keyboard experience.
  • Outdated vulnerabilities (OWASP Top 10 from 2013).
  • Zero excitement or gamification.
