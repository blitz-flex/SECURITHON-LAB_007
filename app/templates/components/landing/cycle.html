The Operational Cycle

How Securithon Lab transforms theory into architectural mastery.

01

Initialize Vector

Select a scenario. The platform instantly spins up an isolated, vulnerable production environment (Containers, Databases, APIs) exclusively for you.

02

Audit & Exploit

Act as the Red Team. Analyze the source code and infrastructure configurations to identify the critical zero-day or architectural flaw.

03

Patch Architecture

Switch to Blue Team. Write the actual code, modify the Nginx configs, or update the IAM policies to permanently close the vector.

04

Automated Verification

Submit your fix. Our backend runs aggressive regression tests and simulated exploits to verify your patch holds under pressure.
